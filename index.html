Mozzerella Sticks

Time to make: 90 minutes

Yield: 15 mozzerella sticks

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups plain Planko
  • 0.5 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 0.5 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 0.25 teaspoon table salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 0.5 cup flour
  • 1.0 pound mozzerella cheese block
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Marinara Sauce

Recipe:

  1. Cursh the Panko bread crumbs until they are fine.
  2. Stir Italian seasoning, parsely, garlic powder, pepper, salt, and panko bread crumbs.
  3. In a seperate bowl, whisk the 2 eggs until they are scrambled.
  4. In a 3rd seperate bowl, pour all of the flour.
  5. Cut the mozzerella cheese block into slices of your desired size, 15 is reccomended size.
  6. Dip each piece of cheese into the flour, then the egg, then fully coat it in the bread crumb mixture.
  7. Once each mozzerella stick is dipped, let them chill in the freezer for about 60 minutes.
  8. Preheat the over to 415F.
  9. Arrange the sticks on a tray and bake them for 10-15 minutes until they turn golden brown.
  10. Use marinara sauce to dip them in
  11. Enjoy the cheesey mozzerella sticks!

Bacon Cheeseburger

Time to make: 35 minutes

Yield: 4 burgers

Ingredients:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 pund ground beef
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 8 slices bacon
  • Other optional toppings - lettuce, tomato, onion, ketchup, etc

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F. Bake the bacon for about 20 minutes, until brown and crispy.
  2. If you are using other toppings, chop up the lettuce, tomatoes, and onions.
  3. Divide the ground beef into 4 portions. Press each ball into a patty and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Coat the bottom of a pan with vegetable oil on medium-high heat.
  5. Add the patties to the pan, and cook each side for about 3 minutes depedning on desired doneness. Place the cheese on the patty when cooking the second side.
  6. Take the bacon out of the oven and let the patties cool.
  7. Assemble the burgers with bacon and other toppings going on top of the patty and cheese.
  8. Your homemade bacon cheeseburgers are done!
