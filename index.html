3 Course Meal Recipe Book

This page gives you recipes for an excellent 3 course meal for your family. For the appetizer, you get mozzarella sticks, a bacon cheeseburger for the entree, and an ice cream sundae for dessert.

Mozzarella Sticks

Time to make: 90 minutes

Yield: 15 mozzarella sticks

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups plain panko bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon table salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese block
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Marinara Sauce

Recipe:

  1. Crush the panko bread crumbs until they are fine.
  2. Stir Italian seasoning, parsley, garlic powder, pepper, salt, and panko bread crumbs.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the 2 eggs until they are scrambled.
  4. In a 3rd separate bowl, pour all of the flour.
  5. Cut the mozzarella cheese block into slices of your desired size, 15 is recommended size.
  6. Dip each piece of cheese into the flour, then the egg, then fully coat it in the bread crumb mixture.
  7. Once each mozzarella stick is dipped, let them chill in the freezer for about 60 minutes.
  8. Preheat the oven to 425F.
  9. Arrange the sticks on a tray and bake them for 10-15 minutes until they turn golden brown.
  10. Use marinara sauce to dip them in
  11. Enjoy the cheesy mozzarella sticks!

Bacon Cheeseburger

Time to make: 35 minutes

Yield: 4 burgers

Ingredients:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 8 slices bacon
  • Other optional toppings - lettuce, tomato, onion, ketchup, etc

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F. Bake the bacon for about 20 minutes, until brown and crispy.
  2. If you are using other toppings, chop up the lettuce, tomatoes, and onions.
  3. Divide the ground beef into 4 portions. Press each ball into a patty and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Coat the bottom of a pan with vegetable oil on medium-high heat.
  5. Add the patties to the pan, and cook each side for about 3 minutes depending on desired doneness. Place the cheese on the patty when cooking the second side.
  6. Take the bacon out of the oven and let the patties cool.
  7. Assemble the burgers with bacon and other toppings going on top of the patty and cheese.
  8. Your homemade bacon cheeseburgers are done!

Ice Cream Sundae

Time to make: 5 minutes

Yield: 1 sundae

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 2 tablespoons caramel syrup
  • 1/4 cup whipped cream
  • 1/2 tablespoon chopped nuts
  • 1 teaspoon sprinkles/jimmies
  • 1 ice cream wafer
  • 1 cherry
  • 3 scoops of your favorite flavor

Recipe:

  1. Grab a large bowl and put 1 scoop of ice cream in.
  2. Drizzle half of the chocolate syrup, caramel syrup, and sprinkles on top.
  3. Add two more scoops of ice cream on top.
  4. Add the whipped cream in a large cone shape.
  5. Drizzle the rest of the chocolate syrup, caramel syrup, and sprinkles on top. Also add the wafers, nuts, and cherry on top.
  6. Enjoy your sugary dessert!
